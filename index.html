Let the digital world take a backseat.

Board to Beers is a Memphis based Board Game Bar and the first of it's kind! This will not be just for avid board game players, but for players of all skill levels, including those that have never played. It's all about putting down the technology and enjoying some fun with friends and family.

The Path To Winning

The 3,000 square foot location will house a library of over 400+ games from the owners personal library, as well as local beers, wines and ciders made right here in Memphis.


In order to make this dream a reality, Board to Beers needs a little help! Click below to check out our Kickstarter campaign to learn more about the bar and location.
